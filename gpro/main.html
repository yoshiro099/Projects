About Us

Global Surge is a 501(c)3 registered organization in the United states, Additionally, Global Surge and the Dr. Gregory S. Lyons Founndation are both registered NGOs (Non Profit Organizations) in the Philippines


Gpro Ministry is part of Global Surge that Exist as a Church planting urban ministry committed to reaching this generation for Christ and transforming them into reproducing disciples that will reach the world. Global Surge provides Worship Centers throughout Metro Manila and Southeast Asia. We aim to present Christ in Creative, Credible, and Caring way to young urban residents and to establish community of believers each Equipped and committed to minister effectively to those around them. Also, we actively pursue the planting of Community Group based churches in local foreign urban centers.

Our Mission

Gpro helps to nurture and support young adults.
To merge and harmonize biblical principles with the secular worlds

Our Vision

To see communities commit to truth in all areas
"Spiritual, Economic, Social, Physical" a hope resulting in global change.

Related Programs

We Offer

Education

GPRO have the right amount of tasks that can fully hone students skills and practicality when it comes to Education, we provide them with the right amount of task that will fully maximize their capabilities in their profession.

Psychology

GPRO promotes the use of Psychology teachings to aid mental health, Psychology students will feel right at home with our On-The-Job Training program that will fully maximize their capabilities in the Psychology field.

Arts and Design

GPRO nourishes young artists to be globally professional by honing their arts and design skills to the fullest and provide them with the maximum amount of support they will need for their profession.

Office Administration

GPRO provides students fit for Office Administration an On-The-Job Training that will suit their needs and nourish their skills that will prepare them for global competence and help in their professional skills.

Business and Marketing

GPRO hones young Business and Marketing student's skills with the right amount of tasks that is best suited for them and help them be ready to be professionaly and globally.

Information Technology

GPRO helps nourish young aspiring IT students to use and hone their skills in the field of Information Techonlogy, we provide them with various tasks that will enhance their work skills for the future and train them to become young professionals.

Accounting and Finance

GPRO helps young students in the field of Accounting and Finance by honing their skills in their respective field by giving them tasks best suited to them and help them become globally professional.

Other Related Discipline

GPRO helps young aspiring students and the likes from various courses and fields to hone their skills and prove that they are more than ready to be globally professional.
